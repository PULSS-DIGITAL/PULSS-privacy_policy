PULSS – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 January 2026

1. Introduction

At Pulss ("we," "us," or "our"), our mission is to provide peace of mind through non-intrusive daily check-ins. To deliver this life-safety service, we must collect and process personal and sensitive information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Pulss mobile application (the “App”) and our Managed Response services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Identification Information

  • Identity Data: Full name and date of birth.
  • Contact Data: Phone number, email address, and home address.
  • Visual Data: Profile photograph used by responders.

2.2 Sensitive & Operational Data (Premium / Managed Service)

  • Location Data: Real-time GPS during check-ins or distress alerts.
  • Medical Data: Conditions, allergies, medications, disabilities (voluntary).
  • Access Data: Key safe codes, gate codes, or entry instructions.
  • Safety Hazards: Pets or property hazards to protect responders.

2.3 Emergency Contact Data

  • Names and phone numbers of your designated contacts.
By providing emergency contact details, you confirm that you have obtained consent from those individuals.

2.4 Technical Data

  • Device model, OS version, battery level, and network signal status.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your daily check-ins.
  • Notify emergency contacts if you fail to check in.
  • Initiate automated verification calls.
  • Dispatch local response partners (Premium service).
  • Assist emergency services during critical incidents.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Performance of Contract: To provide subscribed services.
  • Vital Interests: To protect life or physical safety.
  • Explicit Consent: For medical and special category data.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data.

5.1 Local Response Partners

Shared only during a Level 3 dispatch and only with contracted responders.

5.2 Emergency Services

Information may be shared with police, fire, or ambulance services in life-threatening situations.

5.3 Service Providers

We use trusted providers for infrastructure, messaging, and payments, bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active.
  • Incident Logs: Retained for up to 7 years.
  • Deletion: Non-essential data is deleted upon account closure.

7. Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption (TLS & AES-256).
  • Strict access controls for sensitive data.
  • Role-based visibility during active incidents only.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion (subject to legal obligations).
  • Restrict processing.

Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@pulss.click.

9. Children’s Privacy

Pulss is not intended for direct use by children under 13 without verified parental consent.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this policy and will notify users of material changes via email or in-app notice.
